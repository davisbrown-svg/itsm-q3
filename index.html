What Is ITSM — and Why Are We Here?

IT Service Management (ITSM) is the set of processes and tools organizations use to plan, deliver, manage, and improve IT services to employees and customers. Think: help desk ticketing, incident management, change management, asset management, and self-service portals.

The market is dominated by ServiceNow, with Jira Service Management and Moveworks as challengers. Salesforce is disrupting this space with Agentforce — bringing native AI, CRM unification, and faster ROI to a market that is frustrated with legacy tooling.

Why now? The AI wave is forcing a platform decision: customers must choose between bolting AI onto their existing ITSM (12+ months, high risk) or adopting a platform where AI is already native. Agentforce is the only ITSM platform where the AI is architecturally native — not integrated.

Discovery Questions

Use these to uncover pain and build the business case. Never lead with product — lead with questions.

"When ServiceNow comes up for renewal, what's the conversation in the room?"

Why: surfaces cost frustration, renewal timeline, and internal champions vs. blockers.

"What's your AI roadmap for IT service — and how far along are you in deploying it in ServiceNow?"

Why: exposes the 12-month integration gap and creates urgency around the AI mandate.

"If you could automate one repetitive IT process tomorrow, what would it be and why?"

Why: identifies the highest-value POC use case and gets the customer to self-identify the pain.

"How many Help Desk FTEs do you have, and what does a fully-loaded FTE cost you?"

Why: builds the CFO ROI model — every FTE you automate is $75–100K in hard savings.

"How long did your last ServiceNow upgrade take, and what did it disrupt?"

Why: triggers upgrade pain memories and sets up the 69-day go-live comparison.

"Who else in the business is using Salesforce today — and have you considered what it would mean to consolidate?"

Why: opens the platform consolidation story and identifies internal Salesforce advocates.
